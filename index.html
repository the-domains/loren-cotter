About

Loren is a content creator who specialises in travel, art and fashion. She was the deputy culture editor at the Korea JoongAng Daily, an International New York Times-affiliated national newspaper in Seoul, and currently edits blog posts, articles and product descriptions for brands such as Farfetch, House of Fraser and Engel & Voelkers on behalf of London-based agency Quill. She also freelances as a writer for several magazines, websites and PR firms, including Expedia and Bupa.
