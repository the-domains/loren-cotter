About

Loren is a content creator who specialises in travel, art, fashion and digital nomadism. She was the deputy culture editor at the Korea JoongAng Daily, an International New York Times-affiliated national newspaper in Seoul, and currently lives between Spain, Portugal and the UK, editing pieces for brands like House of Fraser, Screwfix and Mightybell for London-based agency Quill. She has written for Paste Magazine, Seoul Magazine and Korea Magazine.
